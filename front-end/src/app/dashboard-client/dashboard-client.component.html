Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through the "Appointments" section. Select "Book New", choose your department, doctor, and preferred date, then check available slots and book your appointment.

How can I access my medical records?

Your medical records can be accessed in the "Medical Records" section. You can view your prescriptions, lab results, medical history, and vaccination records.

How do I pay my bills online?

You can pay your bills through the "Billing" section. Select the invoice you wish to pay and click "Pay Now" to be directed to our secure payment gateway.

How do I start a telemedicine consultation?

Navigate to the "Telemedicine" section, where you can schedule a video consultation or start a chat with healthcare providers.
